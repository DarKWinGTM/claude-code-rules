# Version 0.1.9 - Strengthened the Research Scout lane as an intensive web-research role

> **Parent Document:** [changelog.md](changelog.md)
> **Current Version:** 0.1.10
> **Session:** 20625882-e064-4b40-a415-8d425cf392dc (2026-05-16)

---

## Changes
- strengthened `design/research-enrichment.design.md` so research is described as deep, topic-specific web research rather than a shallow pass
- clarified that the Research Scout lane should be used when the topic needs serious external support, source comparison, and practicality assessment
- added a stronger output contract for Research Scout so it separates supported fact, best-practice guidance, contested claims, and applicability notes
- updated the capsule version chain to `0.1.9`

## Summary
This version makes the research role more serious and explicit. The plugin concept now assumes that important doctrine-level topics may need a dedicated research lane that does more than gather links: it must test evidence strength, compare sources, and assess whether the proposed direction is practical enough to trust.
